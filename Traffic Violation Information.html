According to the Motor Vehicle Act, common traffic violations and their associated fines in India include:

Overspeeding

Fine ranging from ₹1,000 to ₹2,000 for Light Motor Vehicles (LMV), higher for heavier vehicles.

Driving without a valid license

Fine of ₹5,000.

Driving without insurance

Fine of ₹2,000, with potential for further penalties on repeat offenses.

Not wearing a seatbelt:

Fine of ₹1,000.

Riding a two-wheeler without a helmet

Fine of ₹500 for both rider and pillion passenger.

Driving under the influence of alcohol/drugs

Fine of ₹10,000 and potential imprisonment.

Using a mobile phone while driving:

Fine of ₹1,000.

Jumping traffic signals

Fine of ₹500
